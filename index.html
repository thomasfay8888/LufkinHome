East Texas soil shifts constantly — especially after heavy rain or drought. When the ground moves, your mobile home moves with it. Here are the most common warning signs that your home needs professional releveling. 🚪 Doors That Stick or Won't Close When your frame shifts even slightly, doors and windows bind up. This is one of the earliest and most obvious signs of settling. 📐 Uneven or Sloping Floors If floors feel like you're walking uphill, or furniture tips, the piers underneath have settled unevenly. Don't ignore it — it gets worse. 🔍 Cracks in Walls or Ceilings Cracks along drywall seams, around window frames, or in paneling are your home's way of telling you the structure is under stress. 🛁 Plumbing or Drain Problems Pipes run at angles under your floor. When the home shifts, drains back up and water lines can crack. Leveling often fixes "plumbing" problems. ❄️ Drafts & High Energy Bills An unlevel home creates gaps in skirting and wall joints, letting cold air in and conditioned air out. Your AC and heat run harder than they should. 🏗️ Soft or Spongy Floors If floors feel soft, bouncy, or are starting to sink in spots, piers may have shifted or floor joists may have moisture damage — both are fixable.
